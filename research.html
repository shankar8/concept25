Research

By the year 2025, most of the cutting edge technology that's making news today would be completely realised and applied to our life seamlessly. After analysing the design brief I found out the following areas will have a significant influence on the user experience in cars of 2025:

  • Electric Drivetrain
  • Driverless Technology
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Connectivity and Convergence
  • Display Technology
  • Interaction Technologies
  • Ecosystem

Future is Electric

As it stands today, major automobile companies and many newborn startups have showcased their electric powered vehicle prototypes and have started investing heavily on developing drivetrain and battery technology in collaboration with other technology companies. This is as a result of consumers and regulators putting pressure on automakers due to climate change concerns.

Going Driverless

Once found only in science fiction, self driving cars are about to become reality. They can help reduce road accidents, clear up traffic and provide mobility to more people. Companies both from inside and outside the industry are investing their time and resources into autonomous vehicle research.

Connectivity and Convergence

One core theme of the innovative interior concept is a continuous exchange of information between vehicle, passengers and the outside world. We would be more connected than ever before not just with human beings but with our surroundings.

Screen Technology

The car is growing beyond its role as a mere means of transport and will ultimately become a mobile living space. In 2025, with cars driving itself there is nothing much for the passengers to do. The passengers in self-driving cars use their newly gained free time while travelling for relaxing or working as they please. To enhance this experience automakers will provide larger display units in the interior.

Interaction Technology

Human perceives things happening around with the help of following 5 senses: Sight, Smell, Hearing, Touch, Taste
All these senses are used in normal life to interact. Taking these senses as base and defining the interaction modals with system would result in harmonious interactions. Easy to learn and use natural gestures for interacting with the machine. Sight, touch, hearing are commonly used interaction modals, it would be great to see how smell and taste add value to the future cockpits.

Ecosystem

There are several things which needs to be streamlined to seamlessly integrate the next generation vehicles on our roads. Their adoption depends on regulations, policies, and permits. Regulatory bodies need to lay down rules and regulation for plying all electric and autonomous cars. Infrastructure needs to be updated to match the requirements of technology driven future.

UI Trends

The interface of tomorrows devices will be a lot more personnalized according to the users preference and taste.

Industry Research

All the major automobile and consumer tech companies are spending a lot resources and time to develop cars of the future.
