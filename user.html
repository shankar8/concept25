User

  • Name John Connor
  • Age 32 years
  • Profession Product Photographer
  • Interests Motorsports, Movies, Tennis

User Story - Weekday

  • 7:00am Wakes up
  • 7:05am His virtual assistant Pepa greets him turns on the coffee machine
  • 7:10am John drinks his cup of coffee
  • 7:20am Workout at the gym while Pepa runs through the days schedule
  • 8:00am He ask voice assistant to call for a small car from his car subscription to go to office
  • 8:15am The car arrives just in time when he is ready to leave. When john approaches the car, his face is scanned for authentication and he is let in. Upon authentication all his personal settings are downloaded into the cars onboard computer from the cloud. Pepa asks him if he wants to drive or to be driven. The car figures out the fastest route to office and starts driving on its own.
  • 8:20am He attends his daily morning meeting with his peers who are also driving to office in their cars. His colleague shares a document which he views on the central screen.
  • 9:00am He arrives at his office. He gets logout as soon as he steps out of the car. Car goes to next subscriber. John goes to office.
  • 4:00pm Pepa reminds him that he needs to visit his parents and pick his wife and kids from there. She calls a small car for him.
  • 4:15pm The car picks him up and starts driving to his parents home.
  • 4:25pm The car faces sudden breakdown and gives away a distress call to nearby cars to pick him up.
  • 4:30pm Another car arrives and he continues his journey.
  • 4:40pm He orders desserts using his favourite food delivery app.
  • 5:00pm Arrives at his parents place and have dinner with everyone. He asks Pepa to record the live football to watch later.
  • 8:30pm Pepa calls for a family car to carry everyone to Johns place.
  • 9:30pm Pepa sets home temperature a few minutes before they arrive.
  • 10:00pm John goes to sleep.

User Story - Weekend

  • 7:00am Wakes up
  • 8:00am Ask Pepa to book for a session at the Nurburgring race track and call for a sports car. In drive mode the UI becomes minimal and displays only necessary information and important notifications on the cluster zone.
  • 8:30am The car arrives. John chooses to drive to the track and switches to drive mode, upon which he is asked to verify his driving license by placing his thumb on the steering wheel scanner. This safety feature is there to make sure that drive mode is available for license holding users only.
  • 10:45am At the track, the track mode gets automatically unlocked and John switches to it. He is shown the old lap timings and stats. While going around the track the system shows him tips to improve his performance real time.
  • 11:30am After having some fun at the race track he heads back home in autonomous mode. He asks Pepa to play the match which she had recorded the previous day.
  • 4:00pm Arrived at home and spends evening with family.
  • 10:00pm Goes to sleep

User Scenarios

Looking at the advancements in technology and connectivity, there will be an outburst of possibilities which would be there while travelling in a car:

Few of the scenarios:

  • 1. Making video calls.
  • 2. Attending conference calls
  • 3. Sharing content over call.
  • 4. All sort of entertainment at fingertips : On demand music from cloud Streaming of live events Gaming with emmersive experiences
  • 5. Safe and secure Payments: Toll gate payments Movie ticket bookings Parking payments
  • 6. Communication between cars to help each other during an emergency.
  • 7. Communication between cars and infrastructure would enable endless possibilities.
  • 8. Reading, Resting, Sleeping would be way easy.
  • 9. Planning for trips, parties, dinners etc.
  • 10. Handling of situations like sudden breakdown, medical emergency would be better with the help of tech available.

All these scenarios are like scratching the surface, possibilities are endless and exciting.
